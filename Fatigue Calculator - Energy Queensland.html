Important information

Fatigue level Action required
Low Good to go? Continue to monitor fatigue.
Moderate Discuss with team/crew, decide on appropriate controls. Consider whether high risk tasks should occur. Advise Supervisor if appropriate. Controls may include self and peer monitoring, task rotation, increased breaks, pacing work load.
High Stop Work. Discuss controls with crew/team and with Supervisor. High risk tasks should not be performed. Controls may include increased supervision, task re-assignment, buddy check, arrange back-up, transport alternatives may be required.
Extreme Stop Work (or do not commence). Discuss contingency with supervisor. Controls are unlikely to be sufficient. Make arrangements to convey worker home. Continuing with any work requires approval.
